SUSTANIABLE AGRICULTURAL

Think Green organizes Farmer Field Schools which help provide instruction in basic sustainable techniques and encourage experimentation. Farmers take an active role in improving their agriculture, making change both environmentally and culturally sustainable as they prepare for eventual graduation.
