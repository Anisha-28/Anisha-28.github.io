Green @ Kathleen: Summer Veggie Garden Success!

Tue, 8 Oct, 3:30 pm – 5:00 pm


Location: Kathleen Syme Library and Community Centre, 251 Faraday Street, Carlton, VIC 3053


Anyone who has tasted a home-grown tomato dreams of summer veggie patch success - and this workshop is here to help! We'll talk through the tips and tricks to plant your patch and keep it thriving through the warmer months, including advice about individual crops such as tomatoes, eggplants, cucumbers, zucchinis and capsicums.

This workshop is perfect for those just starting their veggie patch, or who have been gardening for a year or two. We'll focus on taking plants from seeds all the way up to getting the most out of your harvest.
